A top prospect for the 2019 NHL Entry Draft, he was tabbed as "the best German defense prospect since Christian Ehrhoff" by The Hockey Writers. Moritz Seider

Once voted to be the most popular athlete in Detroit sports history, locals often simply refer to him as "The Captian" Steve Yzerman

Nicknamed "Mr. Hockey", this player is often considered the most complete player to ever play the game and one of the greatest of all time. Gordie Howe

He is widely regarded as one of the greatest defencemen in NHL history and nicknamed "The Perfect Human." Nicklas Lidstrom

Prior to turning professional, he played collegiate hockey for the University of Michigan where he was named the Freshman of the Year. He was also selected for the All-Freshman Team, All-Big Ten First Team, and Second Team All-American. Dylan Larkin

He played his entire National Hockey League career with the Detroit Red Wings of the National Hockey League (NHL), for whom he would serve as captain for his final six seasons. Henrik Zetterberg
